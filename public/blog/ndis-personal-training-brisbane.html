Personal Training for NDIS Participants: Creating Inclusive Fitness Goals

Published: June 19, 2025

Fitness should be accessible to everyone, and that's exactly what NDIS funding for personal training makes possible. As an NDIS-registered personal trainer here in Brisbane, I've seen firsthand how transformative proper fitness support can be for participants.

Understanding NDIS Fitness Support

The NDIS recognises that physical fitness is crucial for overall wellbeing, independence, and quality of life. Under the "Improved Health and Wellbeing" category, participants can use their funding for personal training that supports their specific goals and needs.

This isn't about one-size-fits-all fitness – it's about creating programmes that work with your abilities, challenges, and aspirations.

What Makes NDIS Personal Training Different

Individualised Assessment: Every participant starts with a comprehensive assessment that considers their disability, current fitness level, medical considerations, and personal goals. This ensures the programme is both safe and effective.

Adaptive Techniques: Exercises are modified to work with your abilities, not against them. Whether you're managing mobility challenges, chronic conditions, or other disabilities, there's always a way to build strength and improve fitness.

Goal-Focused Approach: Your training goals might include improving daily living activities, building strength for independence, managing pain, or simply feeling more confident in your body. Every programme is designed around what matters most to you.

Ongoing Support: NDIS personal training isn't just about the exercise session – it's about building sustainable habits and providing ongoing encouragement.

Real Benefits for Real Lives

Participants often report improvements in:

  • Daily activities becoming easier
  • Better sleep and energy levels
  • Increased confidence and independence
  • Reduced pain and improved mobility
  • Enhanced mental health and wellbeing

How to Get Started

If you're an NDIS participant interested in personal training, start by discussing it with your support coordinator or planner. Many participants are surprised to learn that fitness support is available through their funding.

The key is finding a trainer who understands the NDIS system and, more importantly, understands how to create inclusive, effective fitness programmes.

Brisbane has excellent resources for NDIS participants, and personal training can be a powerful tool in your journey towards greater independence and wellbeing.
